FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

For direct purchases, we accept Venmo (@sakekittycards) and PayPal (paypal.biz/sakekittycards) — PayPal also supports credit and debit card payments without requiring a PayPal account. For orders through our TCGPlayer store, all major payment methods are accepted through their secure checkout.
Yes, we ship! All cards are shipped in penny sleeves, top loaders, and bubble mailers to ensure they arrive safely. For TCGPlayer orders, shipping is calculated at checkout. For direct purchases, shipping costs will be confirmed when you reach out — typically $1–$5 depending on order size.
We use standard TCG condition grading: Near Mint (NM) — essentially perfect with minimal to no wear; Lightly Played (LP) — minor wear not visible during normal play; Moderately Played (MP) — visible wear but card is still tournament-legal; Heavily Played (HP) — significant wear. Every card is graded conservatively — when in doubt, we grade down.

If a card arrives damaged due to shipping, or if it is significantly different from its listed condition, contact us within 3 days of delivery with photos and we'll make it right — either a full refund or replacement.

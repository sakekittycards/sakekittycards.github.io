FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

At checkout on our shop, we accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay (via Square), plus Venmo and PayPal. For direct payments outside the cart, use Venmo (@sakekittycards) or PayPal (paypal.biz/sakekittycards). Orders through our TCGPlayer store accept all major payment methods via their secure checkout.
Yes, we ship! All cards go out in penny sleeves, top loaders, and bubble mailers so they arrive safe. Direct orders placed through this site are a flat $5 shipping rate — same rule applies to apparel, merch, or anything else we list direct. Future card and sealed packages will ship insured at $1 per $100 of card value (added at checkout, not on apparel/merch). TCGPlayer orders have their own checkout-calculated rates.
We use standard TCG condition grading: Near Mint (NM) — essentially perfect with minimal to no wear; Lightly Played (LP) — minor wear not visible during normal play; Moderately Played (MP) — visible wear but card is still tournament-legal; Heavily Played (HP) — significant wear. Every card is graded conservatively — when in doubt, we grade down.
We personally inspect every card we sell and do our best to catch any fakes, reprints, or proxies before listing. On the rare chance one slips through, reach out immediately — we'll make it right with a full refund or replacement, no hassle.
If a card arrives damaged due to shipping, or if it is significantly different from its listed condition, contact us within 3 days of delivery with photos and we'll make it right — either a full refund or replacement. Buyer satisfaction is our priority. For TCGPlayer orders, their standard buyer protection policy applies.

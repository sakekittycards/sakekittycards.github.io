How It Works

1

Send Us Your Cards

Mail them in or hand them off at an event. Include your name, email, and which service you want on each card.

2

We Evaluate Within 2 Days

Every card gets reviewed against current grading standards for PSA, SGC, and CGC.

3

You Get Your Results

Pass/fail by email. Detailed tier includes photos and written notes on every fail. Prep tier ships cards back submission-ready.

4

Cards Come Back to You

We return your cards securely, or forward them directly to your grading company if you're ready to submit.

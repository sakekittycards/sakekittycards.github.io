Card Prep
Already know which cards you're submitting? Skip screening — pick Prep Only on each card. We'll prep and (if you want) submit through PSA, CGC, or Beckett.
  • Microfiber dust-off — no substances or alterations to the card
  • Placed in a penny sleeve and card saver
  • Submission is optional — we'll submit through PSA / CGC / Beckett on your behalf, OR return the prepped cards for you to submit yourself
  • Available alone (Prep Only) or stacked with Standard / Detailed Screen

Refunded if the card fails screening. When Card Prep is added on top of Standard or Detailed Screen and the card doesn't pass, the $5 prep fee is refunded — we only charge prep on cards we'd actually submit.

Not included in the $5: grading submission fees (set by PSA, CGC, or Beckett based on service tier) and shipping insurance (available at your option).

Mix & Match Per Card

Build your list with whatever service each card needs. Detailed Screen + Card Prep for cards you're unsure about · Standard Screen for quick pass/fail culling · Prep Only for cards you've already vetted and just want submitted. You can mix all three in one request.

🎴

Error Cards Welcome

Miscuts, misprints, wrong backs — all welcome. We send error cards to CGC for their detailed error labeling and handle the submission on your behalf.

How It Works

1

Submit Your List

Use the form below to search each card, choose your service level, and send us your request.

2

We Review & Approve

We review every request before you ship anything. We want to give each card the attention it deserves — capacity can fill up, so we confirm availability first.

3

Send Us Your Cards

Once approved, mail your cards in or hand them off at an event. We confirm receipt and send payment instructions before any work begins.

4

We Evaluate

Each card is reviewed against PSA, CGC, and Beckett standards within 2–3 business days. Standard Screen returns a pass/fail verdict. Detailed Screen adds written notes and photos for every card that fails.

5

Results by Email

Screening results are sent by email. Cards not being submitted for grading can ship back right away or be held and returned together with your graded cards — your choice.

6

Prep, Submit, or Return

Have us submit through PSA, CGC, or Beckett, or get your prepped cards back ready to submit yourself.
✓ Prep fee refunded if it doesn't pass.

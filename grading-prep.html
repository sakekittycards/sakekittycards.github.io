How It Works

1

Submit Your List

Use the form below to search each card, pick your service, and send us your request.

2

We Review & Approve

We'll confirm we can take your submission and follow up with next steps. It's possible we can become overwhelmed, so approval comes first to ensure quality.

3

Send Us Your Cards

Once approved, mail them in or hand them off at an event. We'll confirm receipt and send a payment link.

4

We Evaluate

Every card gets reviewed against current grading standards for PSA, CGC, and Beckett within 2–3 business days.

5

Results & Return

Screening results sent by email. Cards not sent for grading can be returned to you right away or held and shipped back alongside your graded cards — your choice. Card Prep submissions go to the grading company and are returned once graded.

About Us

A crew of collectors with a big love for Pokémon cards.

Our Story

Sake Kitty Cards started the way most card businesses do — with a passion for collecting that grew into something more. What began as one collector hunting that one chase card has grown into a crew of vendors, all chasing the same thing: great cards at honest prices, handled by people who actually care.

We believe the Pokémon card market should feel welcoming and trustworthy, not intimidating. Whether you're chasing a specific alt art, building a budget deck, or just cracking packs for fun — you deserve a vendor who treats every order with care.

Every card we sell is personally inspected, honestly described, and packed to make the trip safely. That's our promise.

Meet the Team

The people behind every card — passionate collectors who know their stuff.

Nick Williams

Founder & Owner

Sake Kitty Cards is built to be a place for collectors — honest on condition, fair on price, and genuinely excited whenever someone finds a card they've been chasing. Nick founded it on the values he grew up collecting with: care over speed, people over transactions. Hands on every card, every time.

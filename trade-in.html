Sell / Trade

Sell bulk, singles, sealed, or graded — cash or store credit, your choice. Build a list below and we'll get back with a quote. English & Japanese Pokémon cards only.

💳
Cash vs. Store Credit — what's the difference?
When you sell, you can take payment as cash or as store credit. Credit pays a higher % than cash on every tier (see the rates below) — the trade-off is that credit only spends with us. We issue store credit as a Square gift card: you get a unique code with your balance loaded onto it, redeemable on anything in the shop (apparel, merch, future drops) and at any of our vendor shows / pop-ups. Anyone at the booth can type your code into our Square POS to verify the balance and apply it directly to your purchase — no manual math.

Want to check your balance between visits? Email or DM us with your code and we'll send back what's on file.

Find Specific Cards

Searches both English (TCGPlayer market data) and Japanese (TCG CSV). Other languages aren't accepted — French, German, Spanish, Chinese, Korean, etc.

Search covers singles and sealed product (booster boxes, ETBs, bundles, tins, etc.) — try something like "Obsidian Flames ETB" or "Evolving Skies Booster Box". You can also search just by the number printed on the card — "170/198", "170", English promo IDs like "SWSH001", "BW17", "TG01", "GG70", "SV01", or Japanese promo numbers like "242/SV-P", "001/S-P", "024/SM-P".

💡
Heads up: this tool is a work in progress. If the search doesn't find a card, or the price looks off, reach out to us and we'll help directly. We also recommend photographing your cards before shipping — it helps us verify everything on intake. If a card ends up being a different variant than what was selected (say Unlimited vs 1st Edition Charizard), pricing will be adjusted to the correct current market value. Final pricing is always based on actual condition and variant at inspection.
